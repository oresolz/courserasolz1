Nigeria's Top 3 Foods

Rice and Stew

Rice and stew is basically eaten every day. Rice should be in the national flag of this country to be very honest. As the name implies. Rice, basic rice is boiled and eaten with stew, which is just tomato sauce that knows css

Bread

What do I have to say ? What do I really have to say ? Bread Bread Bread oh mighty Bread, nothing like Bread if we are being honest. You can literally eat bread with anything even Groundnut fgs. Never underestimate the power of good hot and soft bread early in the morning.

Garri

You have probably never heard of the phrase "Garri no dey get advert" The person that said that didnt lie, Garri has had a chokehold on Nigerian households since we became independent, I'm sure even before we became independent and not once has there been a single advertisement on television to promote Garri. I havent even told you what Garri is yet, its just cassava that learnt javascript.
